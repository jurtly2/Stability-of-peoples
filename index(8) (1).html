The Stability of Peoples

I am rereading Adam Smith’s The Wealth of Nations and answering it with The Stability of Peoples: a plain-language doctrine asking whether wealth creates stable life for people, land, and future generations.

Why are we reading this old book?

The Wealth of Nations helped shape how the modern world talks about wealth, labor, production, markets, trade, growth, taxes, debt, and national success.
